Acupuncture Treatment

Acupuncture Information

Are you nervous about dental treatment?

If you have concerns before starting a treatment, having a relaxing, de-stressing acupuncture session could change the way you feel about dentistry.

Pre-releasing tension and anxiety will help you bond with your clinician and build trust so that with each session you feel increasingly confident. This treatment is also available for patients who have chronic sinus problems, jaw problems and anxiety.

When you call reception to book your appointment, tell our empathetic staff your feelings and concerns, so they may inform Dr Davies and it can be discussed when you come in, before any dental work is started.

Dr Davies will explain what dental acupuncture can do for you and your individual situation or explain to you the other options if more appropriate. It may be that you will need a few sessions before dental treatment commences, or it may be best to have acupuncture whilst you undergo a dental procedure.

Whatever makes you feel more comfortable and relaxed; Dr Davies aims to create long-lasting relationships with her patients, based on trust and open communication.
