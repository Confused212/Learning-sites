Facial Aesthetics

Facial aesthetics

Wrinkles are part of the ageing process as, over time, the structural makeup of your skin alters. As we get older, we start to see two kinds of wrinkles appear. Dynamic wrinkles are those caused by repeated facial movements and may commonly form around the mouth and eyes, while static wrinkles are those caused by the loss of collagen in your body.

Anti wrinkle injections

Anti-wrinkle injections are a fuss-free and straightforward option if you are looking to soften lines and wrinkles, or to lift your brows or jowls. They can also help to slim the lower face and can also be used in conjunction with dermal fillers to create a more natural looking, non-surgical face lift.

Dermal fillers

Dermal fillers plump out fine lines and wrinkles to restore a more youthful appearance. At Rosconnor Specialist Dentistry we use a dermal filler called hyaluronic acid (HA), which is something that already naturally occurs in the body.
